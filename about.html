About Us

Our Story

Tarek AlKhalil Drug Store

Tarek AlKhalil Drug Store was established to support the healthcare sector with reliable pharmaceutical import and distribution services in Syria. From the beginning, our focus has been on quality, compliance, and continuity. We work closely with trusted international suppliers to ensure that all pharmaceutical products meet regulatory standards and are handled with care throughout the supply chain. As a dedicated pharmaceutical Drug Store, our mission is to ensure that essential medicines reach pharmacies and healthcare providers safely, efficiently, and on time, contributing to better healthcare outcomes for the community we serve. Today, Tarek AlKhalil Drug Store continues to grow by building long-term partnerships, maintaining strict quality controls, and adapting to the evolving needs of the pharmaceutical market.
